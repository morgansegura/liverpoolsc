Can I use my 2018 or 2020 Points? (i.e., do I have to use 2019 Points?)

You may only apply your 2019 Use Term Points toward payment for the Owners at Sea cruise. Points from 2018 and 2020 may not be used (for example, 2018 accrued Points may not be used).

When will I see my Points deducted from my account?

Please allow up to 72 hours for your cruise reservation to be reflected in Owner's Lounge.

Do I need to pre-pay my VOA Dues?

You do not need to pre-pay your VOA Dues if you are utilizing Points for the Owners at Sea cruise; however, you will need to ensure your VOA Dues are paid in full no later than January 31, 2019, or that you are current on all VOA Dues payments if you are enrolled in a payment plan. If your VOA Dues are not paid (or if you are not current if you are on a payment plan) by January 31, 2019, your cruise will be cancelled and 100% of the gross fare will be lost, including any Points and/or cash used for payment. If you are travelling with a guest or a Co-Owner, their cruise will also be cancelled without refund.

What if I'm unable to pay my VOA Dues before the cruise?

If your VOA Dues are not paid or are not current by January 31, 2019, your cruise will be cancelled and 100% of the gross fare will be lost. If you are travelling with a guest or a Co-Owner, their cruise will also be cancelled without refund.

What is the cancellation policy?

You have 24 hours after purchasing your cruise to cancel for any reason for a full refund.

After 24 hours, Owners who cancel within the dates shown below for any reason, including medical or family reasons, are subject to the following cancellation fees:

50% of gross fare (applicable to all forms of payment, including Points) for cancellations on or before October 31, 2018

75% of gross fare (applicable to all forms of payment, including Points) for cancellations between November 1 and November 30, 2018

100% of gross fare (i.e., No Refund of any payment, including Points) for cancellations on or after December 1, 2018

Cancellation of an Owner’s Cruise requires, cancellation of the other Owner’s Cruise if there are two Owners on the Title; alternatively, it requires cancellation of the Owner’s guest’s cruise if there is only one Owner on the Title.

Additionally, should you use your 2019 Use Term Points to pay for a portion of your cruise, your 2019 VOA Dues must be paid in full by January 31, 2019, unless you are on a payment plan, in which case you must be current at that time. If this obligation is not met, your cruise will be cancelled and 100% of gross fare will be lost (including any cash or Points). If you are travelling with a guest or a Co-Owner, their cruise will also be cancelled without refund.

Can I bring a guest on the cruise?

This cruise is priced based on a double-occupancy, but it is intended for Welk Resorts Platinum Program Owners. If your Ownership does not have another Owner on Title, you are welcome to bring a guest. Your guest must be over 21. If your Ownership does have another Ownership on Title, the Co-Owner must travel with you on the cruise.
